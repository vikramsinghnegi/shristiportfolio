shristi

Photograher and Creator

Hello, I'm Shristi, a passionate photographer based in the lively city of Lucknow. My journey in photography began with a deep fascination for visual storytelling, and over the years, I've honed my skills to specialize in both portrait and product photography.

Living in Lucknow, I draw inspiration from the city's rich culture and heritage, often incorporating elements of its vibrant surroundings into my work. As a product photographer, I thrive on the challenge of capturing the essence of various products, highlighting their unique features and enhancing their appeal through creative visuals

With each click of my camera shutter, I strive to create images that not only capture moments but also evoke emotions and tell stories. Whether it's a portrait session or a product shoot, I approach every project with dedication and attention to detail, ensuring that the final result exceeds expectations.
